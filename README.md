Your time, your food, instantly. The queue-free revolution.  
What is the Problem?  
The core problem is the friction and loss of time in food transactions within high- 
density environments, such as university campuses and office districts. This  
inefficiency is a bottleneck that negatively affects everyone:  
• For the Customer (Student/Office Worker): Time is the most valuable  
resource, and it is systematically wasted. We are talking about an average wait  
of fifteen to twenty minutes per meal—time that is stolen from studying,  
working, or resting. This wait creates a frustrating, outdated, and stressful  
experience, especially during peak hours.  
• For the Business (Cafeteria/Restaurant): Long lines are an invisible but  
powerful barrier. Many potential customers, upon seeing a waiting line, simply  
turn around and leave, which represents a direct and significant loss of daily  
revenue. Operationally, the chaos of peak hours increases order errors,  
stresses staff, and complicates inventory management, often leading to food  
waste.  
What is the Solution?  
Q-Less is a digital ecosystem that completely eliminates queues and optimizes  
operations through a "Order, Pay, and Pick Up" platform. Our solution differentiates  
itself by integrating a seamless mobile experience with a universal, low-cost 
payment  
system, using the Interledger Protocol (ILP) as its technological cornerstone.  
• Mobile App for Customers (Q-Less Go): An intuitive application where users  
explore menus, customize their orders, pay securely from any compatible  
digital wallet, and, most importantly, schedule the exact pickup time.  
• Control Panel for Businesses (Q-Less Station): A simple web interface that  
works on any tablet or computer. Here, staff receive prepaid orders in real- 
time, organized by pickup time, allowing for an orderly and efficient kitchen  
workflow.  
• Instant Delivery: Upon arriving at the scheduled time, the customer simply  
scans a unique QR code for their order at the counter for instant validation and  
handover. The process of picking up the food takes no more than twenty  
seconds.  
What are the Benefits?  
The benefits are tangible, immediate, and create a virtuous cycle for the entire  
community.  
For the Customer:  
• Massive Time Recovery: We transform minutes of frustrating waiting into  
seconds of convenience. Over an academic year, this translates to giving a  
student back entire days of productive time.  
• Total Control and Flexibility: The user decides when and where to order,  
adapting the service to their lifestyle, not the other way around.  
• Universal and Simple Payments: Thanks to Interledger, users can pay from  
various digital money sources without relying on a specific credit or debit card.  
For the Business:  
• Direct Revenue Increase: By eliminating the barrier of queues, a much higher  
volume of customers can be served in the same amount of time, maximizing  
profitability during peak hours.  
• Drastic Cost Reduction: High commissions from traditional card processors  
are minimized thanks to Interledger's efficiency. Furthermore, kitchen  
optimization reduces costly errors and food waste.  
• Business Intelligence: The platform provides valuable data on customer  
preferences and demand patterns, enabling strategic decisions for business  
growth.  
• Loyalty and Modernization: Offering a superior technological experience not  
only attracts new customers but also builds lasting loyalty in a generation that  
values efficiency and innovation.

2. High-Level Technical Solution Description  
Our architecture is designed to be agile, scalable, and operate in real-time. It 
consists  
Of a mobile application for customers (developed in React Native for iOS and  
Android), a web-based control panel for businesses (in React.js for maximum  
Compatibility), and a robust backend (built with Node.js). The database, Firestore,  
Ensures that orders are reflected instantly from the customer to the business. The 
key  
Component is our payment gateway, which integrates with the Interledger Protocol  
(ILP) through tools like Rafiki. This allows us to process transactions securely,  
Instantly, and with marginal costs, regardless of the user’s digital wallet.  
Authentication and notifications will be managed with Firebase services for a 
secure  
And communicative experience.
